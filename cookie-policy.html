Cookie Policy

How we use cookies and similar technologies on this website

Taranis Capital Limited ("we", "us", "our") uses cookies and similar technologies on taraniscapital.com to improve your experience and understand how you use our website. This Cookie Policy explains what cookies are, which cookies we use, and how you can control them.
Last updated: 2 April 2026

1. What Are Cookies

Cookies are small text files that are placed on your device (computer, tablet, or mobile phone) when you visit a website. They are widely used to make websites work more effectively, and to provide information to website owners. Cookies help the website remember your preferences, understand how you navigate the site, and improve your overall user experience.
There are different types of cookies with different purposes:
  • Session cookies: Temporary cookies that expire when you close your browser
  • Persistent cookies: Cookies that remain on your device for a set period or until manually deleted
  • First-party cookies: Cookies set by the website you're visiting
  • Third-party cookies: Cookies set by other domains, such as analytics or advertising services

3. Strictly Necessary Cookies

These cookies are essential for the website to function properly and provide you with requested services. They cannot be switched off in our systems, as they are set in response to actions you take on our website, such as setting your cookie preferences or logging into a secure area.
Examples of strictly necessary cookies include:
  • Cookie consent preferences
  • Security and fraud prevention measures
  • Session management (keeping you logged in if applicable)
  • Load balancing and basic performance cookies
Strictly necessary cookies do not require your consent under UK and EU law, as they are essential for the website to function.

4. Analytics Cookies

We use analytics cookies to understand how visitors interact with our website. These cookies collect information anonymously and help us compile reports on website traffic, user behaviour, and performance. This information allows us to improve the website and provide a better user experience.
Analytics cookies we may use include:
  • Google Analytics cookies to track page views, user journeys, and engagement metrics
  • Similar analytics services to measure website effectiveness
These analytics cookies are entirely optional. You can opt out of analytics cookies via our cookie banner on first visit, or at any time by managing your cookie preferences. You can also opt out directly through individual analytics providers' tools.

5. Third-Party Cookies

We use third-party services that may set their own cookies on your device:
  • Google reCAPTCHA: Our contact form uses Google reCAPTCHA to prevent spam and abuse. Google may set cookies to verify that you are human. These are governed by Google's Privacy Policy.
  • MailerLite: When you submit our contact form, your information may be processed by MailerLite (our email service provider). MailerLite may set cookies to enhance their services and track campaign effectiveness. These are governed by MailerLite's Privacy Policy.
We are not responsible for the cookies set by third-party services. Please review their privacy policies to understand how they use cookies and what data they collect.

6. Managing Your Cookies

You have full control over cookies on your device. Most browsers allow you to refuse cookies or alert you when a cookie is being set. However, please note that refusing or deleting cookies may affect the functionality of this website and your experience.
Browser Settings: You can control cookies through your browser's settings. Here are brief instructions for common browsers:
  • Google Chrome: Click the menu icon, select Settings, go to Privacy and Security, and then Cookies and Other Site Data.
  • Mozilla Firefox: Click the menu icon, select Settings, go to Privacy & Security, and scroll to the Cookies and Site Data section.
  • Safari: Click Safari in the menu, select Preferences, go to Privacy, and adjust the "Cookies and Website Data" settings.
  • Microsoft Edge: Click the menu icon, select Settings, go to Privacy, Search and Services, and then Cookies and Other Site Data.
You can also delete cookies at any time. Instructions vary by browser, but typically you can access this through your browser's History or Settings menu under "Clear Browsing Data" or "Clear Cookies".

7. Your Consent

When you first visit our website, we display a cookie banner asking for your consent to use cookies. This banner allows you to:
  • Accept all cookies: You consent to all cookies, including analytics cookies.
  • Decline non-essential cookies: You consent only to strictly necessary cookies; analytics and optional cookies will not be set.
You can change your cookie preferences at any time by clearing your browser cookies and revisiting the site, which will display the cookie banner again. Alternatively, you may adjust your preferences directly through your browser settings.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Cookie Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices, technology, legal requirements, or new cookies we may introduce. We will notify you of any material changes by posting the updated policy on our website and updating the "Last updated" date above. Your continued use of our website following any updates constitutes your acceptance of the revised policy.
