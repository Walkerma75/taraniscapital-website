Rayan Al-Karawi

Board Adviser

Rayan Al-Karawi is a Board Adviser at Taranis Capital, bringing over three decades of experience across the financial services industry. His career began as an investment analyst at the Central Bank of Saudi Arabia (SAMA), before evolving into senior roles in investment and corporate banking at Dar Alarkan, Zain KSA, and FAS Holding (now Cenomi).

Rayan is Chairman of Razeen Capital and a founding member of MEIRA Saudi Arabia (the Middle East Investor Relations Association). He is an expert in investor relations, IPOs, sukuk issuances, and corporate restructuring.

His deep knowledge of Saudi capital markets, regulatory frameworks, and institutional investor networks provides Taranis Capital with invaluable insight into fundraising strategy and investor engagement across the Kingdom.
