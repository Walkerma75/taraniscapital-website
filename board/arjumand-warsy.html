Prof. Arjumand Warsy

Scientific Advisory Board Member

Prof. Arjumand Warsy

Scientific Advisory Board Member
arjumand@taraniscapital.com

Prof. Arjumand Warsy is a Scientific Advisory Board member for Taranis Capital's BioTech Growth Fund. She is a distinguished retired King Saud University biochemistry professor with a multi-decade tenure.

She is a leading expert in genetic diseases and molecular genetics research, and a recipient of multiple teaching and research excellence awards. She served as Senior Scientist at the Central Laboratory Female Medical Centre and held positions as Postdoctoral Research Fellow at Birmingham University and Professor of Biochemistry at King Saud University's College of Medicine and College of Science.

Her involvement in the WISE Initiative underscores her dedication to leveraging research on genetic diseases and molecular genetics to foster innovations addressing pressing health challenges in the region.
