Bruno Martorano

Board Adviser

Bruno Martorano

Board Adviser
LinkedIn bruno@taraniscapital.com

Bruno Martorano is a seasoned executive with a successful track record built over 35 years of international banking, fintech industry and finance experience. His career includes executive-level leadership positions in fintech consultancy, neobanks, commercial banking, private banking, foreign direct investments (FDI) and cross-border contract negotiations. His clients have included major international corporations, very wealthy families and governmental institutions.

His multicultural, multilingual background has been valuable to his varied career and in his international postings in New York, Paris, Hong Kong, Dubai and London, where he has managed large teams of professionals on a global basis. A hands-on management style has been key to his ability to launch new projects and initiatives, manage crisis situations successfully, turn around otherwise non-performing teams and businesses, and be a major change leader.

With a traditional banking career culminating as CEO of one of the largest Abu Dhabi banks based in London, Bruno is now dedicating his leadership experience, contacts and executive management expertise to bringing transformation to new fields of industry such as fintech consulting, neobanking, digitisation of the health industry, and renewable energy.

Most recently, as CEO of one of the newest and leading fintech companies based in London and launched in Europe, he has led the development of the bank's vision along with a five-year strategy and business plan, the core set of internal policies and procedures relating to credit, three lines of defence, product strategy and market segmentation, as well as SMF functions' roles and responsibilities. His direct interaction with IT platform providers, management consulting firms and lawyers has been vital to the successful momentum of the project.

Bruno has been invited to speak at several events and lead round-table discussions in various forums around the world throughout his career. He continues to participate in events at the invitation of industry as well as governmental institutions.
