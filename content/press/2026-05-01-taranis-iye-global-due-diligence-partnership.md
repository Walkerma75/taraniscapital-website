Taranis Capital, a DFSA-regulated, multi-jurisdictional investment platform headquartered in the Dubai International Financial Centre (DIFC), today announced a strategic partnership with IYE Global, a specialist investigative firm providing advanced background checks and due diligence investigations for corporate entities, investors, professional advisers, and private clients.

The partnership reflects Taranis Capital&rsquo;s commitment to operating at the highest international standards of governance, transparency, and risk management across its investment activities in the UAE, Saudi Arabia, and other global markets.

## Investigative Capabilities

IYE Global brings deep expertise in structured, evidence-led investigations, supporting informed decision-making and proactive risk mitigation across complex domestic and international matters. Led by an executive team with extensive experience in forensic financial investigation, law enforcement, security services, and international finance, IYE Global delivers in-depth assessments of individuals, corporate entities, and complex financial arrangements.

Through long-standing relationships with international law enforcement agencies and a trusted global intelligence network, IYE Global offers unique investigative capabilities, including the identification and assessment of both first- and third-party risks. These investigations are designed to uncover potential links to criminal, fraudulent, or suspicious activities that may impact reputational standing, regulatory exposure, or the integrity of future commercial and professional engagements.

## Scope of the Partnership

Under the partnership, IYE Global will support Taranis Capital across enhanced pre-investment due diligence, counterparty assessments, governance reviews, and ongoing risk monitoring &mdash; ensuring that investment decisions are underpinned by rigorous, independent intelligence and aligned with regulatory expectations.
