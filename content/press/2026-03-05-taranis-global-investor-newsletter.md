Taranis Capital today announces the launch of its first exclusive, invitation-only Global Investor Newsletter, created in response to sustained demand from investors across multiple jurisdictions seeking deeper insight into the firm&rsquo;s expanding platform and fund activations.

As Taranis Capital moves toward the full operational fulfillment of its flagship funds across Data Centres, Biotech, Disruptive Technology, Green Biotech, and Real Estate verticals, the newsletter will provide selected investors with direct, structured, and timely intelligence from the leadership team.

The Global Investor Newsletter will include:

&bull; Strategic updates on fund activations and capital commitments

&bull; Sector-specific market intelligence across GCC, UK, Europe, and Asia corridors

&bull; Regulatory and structural milestones

&bull; Portfolio pipeline visibility

&bull; Executive commentary and forward-looking outlook

This curated communication channel is strictly limited to qualified and sophisticated investors, institutional partners, and select family offices. Distribution will remain controlled to preserve confidentiality, alignment, and strategic positioning.

> The launch of our invitation-only Global Investor Newsletter reflects both the strength of our investor relationships and the significant momentum we are experiencing as we approach full fund activation. We are committed to providing clarity, transparency, and strategic insight to those aligned with our long-term vision. This platform ensures our global investor community remains informed, engaged, and positioned alongside us as we scale.
>
> — Nicholas S. Bingham, Founding Partner &amp; CEO, Taranis Capital

The initiative reinforces Taranis Capital&rsquo;s commitment to disciplined communication, governance transparency, and strategic alignment with its investor base as the firm progresses into its next phase of growth.
