Investment Vehicles

Strategic investment across high-growth sectors

Taranis Capital operates specialised funds with disciplined capital deployment and deep sector expertise. Our investment vehicles are designed to target transformative opportunities across fintech, biotechnology, digital infrastructure, and property — focusing on markets with significant growth potential and alignment with emerging market development.

Fintech Fund

FINANCIAL TECHNOLOGY

Investing in financial technology innovation across payments, digital banking, blockchain, and regulatory technology in emerging markets.

  • Digital payments infrastructure
  • Open banking and API platforms
  • Regulatory technology (RegTech)
  • Blockchain and digital assets
Learn more about the Fintech Fund

Biotech Fund

BIOTECHNOLOGY

Advanced biotechnology platforms focused on knowledge transfer, localisation, and capability building aligned with Saudi Vision 2030.

  • Gene therapy and precision medicine
  • Biotech infrastructure and manufacturing
  • Knowledge transfer and localisation
  • Research and development partnerships
Learn more about the Biotech Fund

Datacentre Fund

DIGITAL INFRASTRUCTURE

Supporting the digital infrastructure backbone across the MENA region, investing in scalable, sustainable datacentre operations.

  • Hyperscale datacentre development
  • Edge computing infrastructure
  • Sustainable cooling and energy solutions
  • Cloud services and connectivity
Learn more about the Datacentre Fund

Property Fund

REAL ESTATE

Strategic property investments in high-growth urban centres, with a focus on commercial and mixed-use developments across emerging markets.

  • Commercial property development
  • Mixed-use urban projects
  • High-growth market positioning
  • Sustainable building practices
