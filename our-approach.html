How We Invest

Disciplined capital deployment guided by ethical governance and deep sector expertise

A principled approach to venture capital

Taranis Capital operates with a distinctive investment philosophy centred on ethical governance and disciplined capital deployment. We believe that companies built on strong ethical foundations deliver superior long-term returns whilst creating positive social impact.

Our focus on emerging markets across MENA, Africa, and Europe reflects our conviction that transformative value creation occurs where economic growth converges with unmet market needs. We combine deep sector expertise with active partnership models that extend beyond capital provision.

We take a patient capital approach, prioritising sustainable growth and knowledge transfer over short-term gains. Our team works alongside portfolio companies to provide strategic guidance, market access, and ecosystem support that accelerates value creation for all stakeholders.

Our Investment Principles

Six core principles that guide our investment decisions and active partnership with portfolio companies.

Ethical Governance

We evaluate governance standards, social impact, and ethical practices alongside financial returns.

Sector Expertise

Deep domain knowledge in fintech, biotech, datacentres, and property enables informed decision-making.

Emerging Market Focus

Targeting high-growth opportunities across MENA, Africa, and Europe where transformation creates value.

Active Partnership

Working alongside portfolio companies with strategic guidance, market access, and ecosystem support.

Knowledge Transfer

Supporting localisation of global expertise and capability building in target markets.

Long-Term Value

Patient capital approach focused on sustainable growth over short-term gains.

Our Investment Process

A disciplined five-stage approach to identifying, assessing, and supporting high-growth ventures.

1

Origination

Identifying opportunities through our network, sector research, and ecosystem partnerships. We focus on emerging market opportunities aligned with our sector expertise and ethical investment criteria.

2

Due Diligence

Comprehensive assessment of financial, operational, governance, and ethical factors. We conduct deep-dive analysis of market dynamics, competitive positioning, management quality, and alignment with our values.

3

Structuring

Designing investment structures aligned with growth objectives and governance standards. We work with management teams to establish clear governance frameworks, performance milestones, and value creation plans.

4

Active Support

Providing strategic guidance, board representation, and ecosystem connections. Beyond capital, we bring network, expertise, and market access to support portfolio company growth and address key challenges.

5

Value Realisation

Supporting exits that maximise value for all stakeholders through disciplined planning. We work proactively with companies and partners to achieve optimal outcomes for investors and portfolio company teams.

We believe companies built on strong ethical foundations and disciplined governance deliver superior long-term returns.
