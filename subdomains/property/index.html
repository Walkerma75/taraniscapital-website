Fund Overview

Taranis Capital's Property Fund deploys capital into strategic real estate investments across high-growth urban centres in the MENA region and emerging markets.

A Disciplined Approach to Real Estate Investment

We focus on commercial and mixed-use developments where rapid urbanisation, economic diversification, and infrastructure development create compelling real estate dynamics. Our investment thesis prioritises properties positioned to benefit from structural economic shifts in key markets.

We combine rigorous financial analysis with deep regional knowledge of the GCC and select emerging markets. Taranis Capital is a DIFC-regulated fund manager authorised by the Dubai Financial Services Authority (DFSA), bringing institutional discipline and global networks to real estate investing.
