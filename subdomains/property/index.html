Fund Overview

Taranis Capital's Property Fund focuses on strategic investments in commercial and mixed-use developments across high-growth urban centres. We target opportunities where rapid urbanisation, economic diversification, and infrastructure development create compelling real estate dynamics.

Our approach combines rigorous financial analysis with deep regional knowledge. We identify properties and developments positioned to benefit from structural economic shifts in key markets, with particular emphasis on the GCC region and selective emerging markets.

Market Opportunity

The GCC property market is experiencing a structural shift driven by economic diversification away from oil dependence. Population growth, rising foreign investment, and major infrastructure projects are creating unprecedented demand for quality commercial and mixed-use real estate.

Dubai continues to set records for transaction volumes and property valuations, whilst Saudi Arabia's Vision 2030 programme is creating massive demand for world-class commercial property, hospitality, and mixed-use developments. The Kingdom is investing heavily in new business districts, tourism infrastructure, and financial centres that require significant real estate investment.

Beyond the Gulf, high-growth markets in Asia and Africa are seeing similar dynamics: infrastructure mega-projects, tourism expansion, financial sector growth, and urbanisation are driving a structural shift in real estate fundamentals. These markets present compelling opportunities for experienced, well-connected investors who can navigate local dynamics and identify assets positioned for long-term appreciation.
