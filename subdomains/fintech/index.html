Fund Overview

Taranis Capital's Fintech Fund deploys capital into financial technology companies at the forefront of digital transformation across emerging markets.

A Disciplined Approach to Fintech Investment

We invest in companies transforming financial services through technology. Our focus spans digital payments infrastructure, open banking platforms, regulatory technology solutions, and blockchain-enabled financial products—sectors reshaping how billions of people access and manage financial services.

Taranis Capital is a DIFC-regulated fund manager authorised by the Dubai Financial Services Authority (DFSA). We bring institutional discipline, global networks, and deep operational expertise to fintech investing.

The Market Opportunity

Fintech is reshaping financial services globally, with emerging markets representing the largest untapped opportunity.

24%

Growth in UK Fintech

UK fintech investment grew 24% in H1 2022, demonstrating sustained investor appetite for financial technology innovation across mature markets.

Emerging Markets Potential

Emerging markets represent a far larger opportunity: billions of unbanked and underbanked individuals gaining access to digital financial services for the first time.

GCC Regulatory Support

The GCC is actively investing in digital financial infrastructure, with governments mandating open banking regulations, digital payment systems, and digital asset frameworks.

Vision 2030 & Beyond

Regional governments (Saudi Arabia, UAE, others) have committed to Vision 2030 initiatives, channelling investment into financial technology, digital banking, and fintech innovation hubs.

Investment Focus

We focus on four core areas transforming financial services in emerging markets.

Digital Payments Infrastructure

Payment processors, settlement networks, and infrastructure enabling frictionless, secure transactions across borders and channels.

Open Banking & API Platforms

API-first platforms unlocking data access, enabling third-party innovation, and accelerating the shift from closed to open financial services.

Regulatory Technology (RegTech)

Software solutions helping financial institutions automate compliance, strengthen AML/KYC processes, and navigate evolving regulatory requirements.

Blockchain & Digital Assets

Technologies enabling decentralised finance, tokenisation, blockchain-based settlement, and central bank digital currencies (CBDCs).
