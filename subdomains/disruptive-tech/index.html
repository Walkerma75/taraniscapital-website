Disruptive Tech Fund

A DFSA-acknowledged venture capital fund investing in disruptive technology companies across AI, cybersecurity, and next-generation platforms

Fund Overview

Taranis Disruptive Tech Fund LP is a closed-ended Qualified Investor Venture Capital Fund established in DIFC and regulated by DFSA. Managed by Taranis Capital Limited (DFSA-regulated fund manager), the fund invests in early-stage and growth-stage companies with innovative technology components and strong global ambitions. We seek companies with exceptional growth potential, strong management teams, defensible competitive advantages, and scalable business models.

Investment Strategy

Capital Allocation

Our investment strategy focuses on capital deployment across development stages with a clear allocation framework:

Series A Investments 35-45%
Series B/C Investments 55-65%

Series A investments focus on companies with proven product-market fit ready to scale. Series B/C investments target companies with proven business models looking to expand reach and market penetration.

Global Mandate & Regional Focus

We operate with a truly global mandate with no geographic restrictions. Our investment team actively sources across major technology hubs in North America, Europe, Asia-Pacific, and the Middle East.

DIFC Advantage

Our position in DIFC combined with deep GCC relationships creates a natural advantage for identifying and supporting technology companies looking to establish or expand Gulf region operations.

Value-Add Approach

Beyond capital, we provide strategic guidance, operational expertise, and access to our extensive network of advisers, limited partners, and technology sector specialists to support portfolio company growth.

GCC Regional Opportunity

The Gulf Cooperation Council region presents a compelling investment opportunity driven by government-led modernisation initiatives with technology at the core.

  • Saudi Arabia Vision 2030: A transformational national strategy focused on economic diversification with significant investment in technology, digital infrastructure, and innovation.
  • UAE Operation 300bn: An ambitious economic initiative emphasising advanced technology, cybersecurity, digital transformation, and emerging industries.
  • Qatar National Vision 2030: Strategic focus on sustainable economic growth with technology and innovation as critical enablers.

Trillions in Government-Directed Modernisation

GCC governments are directing trillions in capital toward technology-enabled modernisation. Companies that successfully establish or expand Gulf operations benefit from substantial local demand, strategic partnerships with government entities, and access to regional capital.

We actively encourage our portfolio companies to evaluate GCC market opportunities and support them in establishing regional operations and partnerships.

ESG Integration

Environmental, social, and governance considerations are integrated throughout our investment process and portfolio management.

Pre-Investment Assessment

Each prospective investment undergoes rigorous ESG evaluation to identify material risks and opportunities. We assess governance structures, management experience, diversity, and environmental impact across all portfolio candidates.

Post-Investment Monitoring

Portfolio companies receive quarterly ESG monitoring and guidance. We work actively with management teams to strengthen governance, expand diversity initiatives, and implement sustainability frameworks aligned with global best practices.

Portfolio Company Support

We encourage and support portfolio companies in adopting recognised ESG frameworks and reporting standards. Access to our board advisers and network of ESG specialists enables portfolio companies to strengthen governance and operational practices.
