Fund Overview

Taranis Disruptive Tech Fund LP is a closed-ended Qualified Investor Venture Capital Fund established in DIFC and regulated by DFSA.

Venture Capital for Disruptive Technology

Taranis Disruptive Tech Fund LP is a closed-ended Qualified Investor Venture Capital Fund established in DIFC and regulated by DFSA. Managed by Taranis Capital Limited, the fund invests in early-stage and growth-stage companies with innovative technology components and strong global ambitions.

We seek companies with exceptional growth potential, strong management teams, defensible competitive advantages, and scalable business models.
