Fund Overview

Taranis Capital's Datacentre Fund focuses on funding the planning, construction, and operation of reliable data centres in emerging economies. Digital infrastructure is critical to driving economic development and remains a fundamental enabler of modern business.

The Fund targets projects enabling rapid deployment of data centre capacity—from hyperscale facilities serving multinational cloud providers to edge computing infrastructure supporting regional connectivity needs. Our approach combines patient capital with deep expertise in infrastructure development and established regional partnerships across the Middle East, Africa, and adjacent markets.

We invest across the full spectrum of data centre development: greenfield site identification and permitting, construction and fit-out, equipment procurement, and operational deployment. Our team brings together proven sector knowledge, financial discipline, and strong on-the-ground relationships to deliver projects that drive economic impact and sustainable returns.

Investment Focus Areas

The Fund targets high-impact data centre infrastructure across four strategic pillars

Hyperscale Datacentre Development

Large-scale facility construction serving multinational cloud providers, major tech companies, and regional hyperscalers. Projects ranging from 5–50+ MW of power.

Edge Computing Infrastructure

Distributed data centre capacity deployed closer to end users, reducing latency and supporting real-time applications, autonomous systems, and 5G/6G networks.

Sustainable Cooling & Energy

Advanced cooling systems, renewable energy integration, and power efficiency technologies that reduce operational environmental footprint and energy costs.

Cloud Services & Connectivity

Co-location facilities, cloud edge nodes, and integrated connectivity platforms supporting enterprise applications, regional cloud services, and digital transformation.

Environmental, Social & Governance

Responsible infrastructure investment is core to our approach

Energy Efficiency

We prioritise data centre designs that minimise power consumption through advanced cooling systems, efficient power distribution, and intelligent workload management. Lower energy use reduces both operational costs and environmental impact.

Renewable Energy Integration

Our projects incorporate renewable energy sources where possible, including solar and wind partnerships. This supports global decarbonisation goals whilst providing sustainable, predictable energy costs for operations.

Sustainable Cooling Solutions

We deploy innovative cooling technologies including free cooling, liquid cooling, and waste heat recovery systems. These reduce water consumption and environmental impact whilst maintaining optimal facility performance.

Community Impact

Data centre development creates local employment, skills training, and economic development opportunities. We work closely with communities and governments to ensure projects deliver broad-based economic benefits.

Governance & Transparency

We maintain rigorous governance standards, transparent reporting, and regular stakeholder engagement. Our advisory board brings world-class expertise and independent oversight to Fund operations and investments.

Digital Inclusion

Reliable data centre infrastructure supports digital inclusion, fintech access, and technology adoption across emerging markets. We believe this is fundamental to sustainable economic development and shared prosperity.
