Fund Overview

Taranis Capital's Datacentre Fund focuses on funding planning, construction, and operation of reliable data centres in emerging economies.

Infrastructure for the Digital Economy

The Fund targets projects enabling rapid deployment of data centre capacity—from hyperscale facilities to edge computing infrastructure.

The Market Opportunity

Digital infrastructure is experiencing unprecedented demand, with emerging markets representing the largest untapped opportunity.

$2T+

Global Market TAM

The global data centre market exceeds $2 trillion in 2024, with annual growth rates of 10-12%, driven by cloud adoption, AI, and digital transformation.

500+

New Developments

More than 500 significant data centre developments were announced globally in 2023, reflecting unprecedented investor appetite for digital infrastructure capacity.

$70B+

Private Equity Investment

Private equity investment in data centre infrastructure grew from $30 billion in 2020 to over $70 billion in 2023, demonstrating accelerating institutional capital deployment.

AI & Cloud Demand

Cloud computing, AI workloads, and fintech infrastructure are driving exponential capacity demand. Emerging markets lack adequate capacity to serve these sectors—creating significant investment opportunity.

MENA Focus

MENA and Africa are experiencing accelerating demand for hyperscale capacity. Regional data residency requirements, growing digital economies, and tech-sector investment are fueling infrastructure development.

GCC Investment

Gulf governments are actively investing in domestic digital infrastructure. Vision 2030 initiatives and regulatory frameworks are creating incentive structures favourable to data centre development and operation.

Investment Focus

We focus on four core infrastructure categories transforming digital capability in emerging markets.

Hyperscale Datacentre Development

Large-scale facility construction, 5-50+ MW of power capacity. Serving multinational cloud providers, regional hyperscalers, and enterprise customers requiring significant compute and storage infrastructure.

Edge Computing Infrastructure

Distributed capacity deployed closer to end users for real-time applications, 5G/6G connectivity, and latency-sensitive workloads. Critical for emerging market IoT, autonomous systems, and mobile computing.

Sustainable Cooling & Energy

Advanced cooling technologies, renewable energy integration, and power efficiency solutions. Supporting sustainable data centre operations while reducing operational costs and environmental impact.

Cloud Services & Connectivity

Co-location facilities, cloud edge nodes, and connectivity platforms connecting data centres to cloud networks, submarine cables, and regional backbone infrastructure.

ESG & Responsible Investment

We are committed to building sustainable, responsible digital infrastructure across all portfolio companies.

Energy Efficiency

Prioritise designs minimising power consumption. Advanced cooling systems, efficient power distribution, and optimal hardware utilisation reduce operational costs and environmental impact.

Renewable Energy Integration

Incorporate renewable energy sources into data centre operations. Solar, wind, and other renewables reduce carbon footprint and provide long-term energy cost stability.

Sustainable Cooling Solutions

Innovative cooling including free cooling, liquid cooling, and water-efficient systems. Reduces water consumption and environmental impact while improving operational efficiency.

Community Impact

Local employment, skills training, and economic development. Data centre projects create jobs and opportunity in emerging markets while building local technical capability.

Governance & Transparency

Rigorous governance standards and transparent reporting. Portfolio companies maintain high ethical standards, regulatory compliance, and stakeholder accountability.

Digital Inclusion

Reliable infrastructure supports fintech access, technology adoption, and digital inclusion. Data centre capacity enables emerging markets to participate in digital economy and access cloud services.
