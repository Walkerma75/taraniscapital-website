Property

Strategic property investments in high-growth urban centres

Sector Overview

Real estate across the MENA region is undergoing a period of exceptional growth, driven by rapid urbanisation, economic diversification, and infrastructure development. Taranis Capital has deep expertise in the property sector, with a focus on commercial and mixed-use developments across high-growth urban centres. Our approach combines rigorous market analysis with deep regional knowledge, enabling us to identify properties and developments that align with the broader economic transformation agendas of the markets we operate in.

Market Opportunity

The GCC property market continues to demonstrate exceptional growth, driven by economic diversification programmes, population growth, and increased foreign investment. Dubai's real estate market has seen record transaction volumes, with residential and commercial segments showing strong demand. Saudi Arabia's Vision 2030 is creating unprecedented demand for commercial, industrial, and mixed-use developments across the Kingdom. The combination of infrastructure mega-projects, tourism expansion, and financial sector growth is driving a structural shift in property market dynamics across the region.

Our Approach

We take a disciplined approach to property investment, focusing on locations with strong fundamentals: proximity to transport infrastructure, growing commercial hubs, and areas benefiting from government-led development initiatives. Our team works closely with local developers, operators, and regulatory bodies to ensure every investment meets our standards for quality, sustainability, and long-term value creation.

By identifying opportunities early in emerging economic centres, we position investors to benefit from structural growth whilst contributing to the development of vibrant, inclusive urban environments.

Ethics & ESG

All property investments are assessed against our ESG criteria, including energy efficiency standards, sustainable building practices, community impact, and responsible development principles. We believe that properties built to the highest environmental and social standards deliver superior long-term returns for our investors.

We actively engage with developers and property managers to embed sustainability into design, construction, and operations. This includes sourcing responsibly, minimising waste, optimising energy consumption, and ensuring developments contribute positively to the communities in which they are located. Our commitment to responsible property development attracts quality tenants, enhances asset value, and positions our portfolio for long-term success.
