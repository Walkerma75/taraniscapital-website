About Taranis Capital

An ethically focused investment fund

Building ventures through disciplined capital and knowledge leadership

Taranis Capital is a venture capital and strategic investment firm with a clear focus on ethical governance and emerging market growth. We invest in high-growth sectors including fintech, biotechnology, datacentres, and property across the MENA region, Africa, and Europe.

Our approach combines deep sector expertise with a commitment to social wellbeing and responsible governance. We believe that companies built on strong ethical foundations deliver superior long-term returns.

We work alongside our portfolio companies as active partners, providing strategic guidance, market access, and ecosystem support to accelerate growth and create lasting value.

Our Values

What guides our investment approach

Ethical Governance

Governance, ethics, and the projects companies work on are all important factors in how we assess investments. We hold ourselves and our portfolio to the highest standards.

Emerging Market Focus

We see transformative potential in markets across the MENA region, Africa, and beyond. Our deep local knowledge and global networks position us to capture this growth.

People & Wellbeing

How staff are treated and social wellbeing matter to us. We invest in companies that value their people and contribute positively to the communities they serve.

Knowledge Transfer

We support the localisation of global expertise and capability building, creating sustainable ecosystems that outlast individual investments.

Ethical governance, how staff are treated, the staff's social wellbeing and the projects companies work on are all important factors in our investment decisions.
